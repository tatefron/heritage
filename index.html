Who we are

We are a charitable foundation dedicated to the development of cultural and social capital in Ukraine with a particular focus on education and arts.

Ukraine is at the forefront of the global war for democracy and food security.

We raise important topics of environment, education, art history not only in Ukrainian, but also in the world community.

What we do

  • Inviting prominent intellectuals, philosophers and experts to work together in Ukraine to stand against fake information, populism, resentment

  • Support gifted Ukrainian artist

We support art

Ada Rogovtseva is a Hero of Ukraine and the most adored actress. She stays in Kyiv, helping all people who went to war, including her grandson and son-in-law. She plays in theatre to support people emotionally and spiritually. She meets with journalists of many counties to tell them about animal crimes of russians and the bravery of our soldiers.

Documentory film Wind from East (Viter zi shody) was filmed during 2014 - 2021 both near the war zone and in the theater. Premier was planned for March 2022. Among actors are Ada Rogovtseva, Ahtem Sietablaev and others. Many of them are fighting in the army now.

Our projects

Here you can see the projects in which our team is involved

Hucho

We provide a network platform for R&D, best-practice & leadership development on Water Security, Sustainable Finance, & EESG. We are solvіng complex water & EESG challenges in the simplest and most effective way to bridge the multi-level gaps between government, society, and industries in Ukraine & other countries.

Green Education Ukraine

New Green School Concept is: New philosophy, embodied in education policy , and institutions independent from the state; Life Sciences topics primarily ; Additional topics delivered in partnership: Computer Sciences, Foreign Languages, Communication etc; R&D in close cooperation with businesses in every cluster; Funded by an Endowment Fund, initially created by businesses;

Youth Leadership Network (YLN)

The Youth Leadership Network (YLN) is a state-wide program promoting self-advocacy and self-awareness for youth and young adults with disabilities ages 14-26. The YLN is run by Easter Seals-MA, and carries out activities through monthly meetings, events and social media.

Borysthenes

Borysthenes initiative is developing practices aimed at building horizontal networks, trust, cooperation between members of Ukrainian society. Through study circles, meetings in small groups, through improving standards for deliberative assemblies and other formats
